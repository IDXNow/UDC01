Description

Nestled on 1.8 acres of prime waterfront real estate, this stunning contemporary masterpiece offers unparalleled luxury living with breathtaking panoramic views of Crystal Lake. Built in 2018 by award-winning architect Michael Reynolds, this 6,450 square foot residence seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor living spaces.

The grand entrance welcomes you with soaring 20-foot ceilings and a wall of windows showcasing the spectacular water views. The main level features an open concept design with a gourmet kitchen equipped with top-of-the-line Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances, custom cabinetry, and an oversized island perfect for entertaining.

The primary suite is a true retreat, offering a private balcony, luxurious bathroom with heated floors, freestanding soaking tub, and a custom walk-in closet. Four additional en-suite bedrooms provide ample space for family and guests.

Outdoor amenities include an infinity-edge pool and spa, covered outdoor kitchen, multiple terraces, professionally landscaped grounds, and a private dock with boat lift. Additional features include a home automation system, wine cellar, home theater, fitness room, and 3-car heated garage.

Located in the prestigious North Shore community, this exceptional property offers privacy while being just minutes from downtown Crystal Lake, fine dining, shopping, and the Crystal Lake Country Club.
